The Name

Why "Rozeta"?

The Rosetta Stone was the artifact that made an undecipherable language understandable for the first time. It didn't create a new language - it translated one that already existed so people could finally access knowledge that had been locked away.

That's Rozeta Labs in one word. AI is powerful, but it's locked behind jargon, complexity, and tools that weren't built for people who run small businesses. We're the translation layer.

Our Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to close the AI resource gap between small businesses and large enterprises. We believe SMB operators deserve access to the same level of execution, automation capability, and operational leverage that bigger companies already use to scale efficiently and compete at a high level.

SMB operators should not be locked out of enterprise-level automation because of hiring constraints, budget limits, or technical complexity. We make that capability accessible through done-for-you implementation and ongoing optimization. By embedding with operators and building practical systems inside real workflows, we help teams reduce manual drag, move faster, and create compounding gains month after month.

The Bigger Picture

Where This Goes.

Rozeta Labs is building the category-defining AI and automation partner for SMB operators. The vision is simple: make enterprise-level execution accessible to businesses that do not have enterprise budgets, enterprise headcount, or enterprise internal teams.

Instead of selling one-off projects or generic advice, we are building a durable operating model: assessment-first prioritization, then ongoing implementation and optimization on retainer.

Over time, this becomes an installed operating layer inside each client business - workflows that keep running, data that keeps improving, and systems that compound in value month after month.
