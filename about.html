The Name

Why "rozeta"?

The Rosetta Stone was the artifact that made an undecipherable language understandable for the first time. It didn't create a new language — it translated one that already existed so people could finally access knowledge that had been locked away.

That's the entire company in one word. AI is powerful, but it's locked behind jargon, complexity, and tools that weren't built for people who run small businesses. We're the translation layer.

Our Mission

Our Mission

To make AI accessible, actionable, and measurable for every small and medium business in America — starting with the operators who need it most and expanding until the translation reaches every employee in every SMB that's ready to move.

We believe AI is going to separate the businesses that thrive over the next decade from the ones that get left behind. We also believe the people currently "helping" small businesses adopt AI are mostly selling tools rather than solving problems. rozeta labs exists to close that gap — not by dumbing things down, but by meeting operators where they are and translating AI into their world.

The through-line: Greg knows what it's like to run a business with real employees, real overhead, and real consequences. That's why the translation lands differently coming from him. He's not teaching theory. He's showing you what actually works when everything is on the line.
