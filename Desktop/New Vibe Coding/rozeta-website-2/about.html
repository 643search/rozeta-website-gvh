The Name

Why "Rozeta"?

The Rosetta Stone was the artifact that made an undecipherable language understandable for the first time. It didn't create a new language - it translated one that already existed so people could finally access knowledge that had been locked away.

That's Rozeta Labs in one word. AI is powerful, but it's locked behind jargon, complexity, and tools that weren't built for people who run small businesses. We're the translation layer.

Our Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to create the world's leading community that helps make AI accessible, actionable, and measurable for every small and medium-sized business in America - starting with the operators who need it most and expanding until the translation reaches every employee in every SMB that's ready to move.

We believe AI is going to separate the businesses that thrive over the next decade from the ones that get left behind. We also believe the people currently "helping" small businesses adopt AI are mostly selling tools rather than solving problems. rozeta labs exists to close that gap - not by dumbing things down, but by meeting operators where they are and translating AI into their world.

The Bigger Picture

Where This Goes.

Rozeta Labs is not just a community. The long-term vision is to become the category-defining company for AI enablement in small and medium businesses. The window for owning this space is 12-18 months. Right now, nobody owns it.

There are generic AI consultants, expensive enterprise firms, and self-serve AI tools - but there is no trusted, operator-first brand that combines education, community, and implementation support in a way that feels like it was built by people who actually understand the industry.

The roadmap includes certification programs, a licensing ecosystem for independent AI consultants, proprietary software tools, and platform partnerships with the major software companies that already serve our target market. The community and consulting are the foundation. Everything else gets layered on top.
