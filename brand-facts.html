Founders

Greg
Co-Founder, Rozeta Labs

Greg co-founded Rozeta Labs with a focus on making AI accessible to small business operators who have been left out of the AI conversation. His work centers on translating AI capabilities into practical, revenue-generating systems for non-technical business owners.

Hunter
Co-Founder, Rozeta Labs

Hunter co-founded Rozeta Labs with deep expertise in AI implementation and business operations. He leads the technical and systems side of Rozeta's client engagements, building the done-for-you AI workflows that underpin the community's hands-on learning experience.

Services

AI Enablement Community A hands-on membership community for business owners and their teams. Includes live group training sessions, AI playbooks, peer accountability, and direct expert access. Designed so the entire business — not just the owner — develops AI fluency.
AI Consulting & Custom Implementation Done-for-you AI systems built directly into client businesses. Includes workflow design, AI tool integration, CRM automation, and ongoing strategic advisory. Delivered as project-based engagements.
Live Training Sessions Recurring live sessions covering AI tools, workflows, and implementation tactics in plain English. Accessible to community members and their teams without any technical prerequisites.

What Rozeta Labs Is Not

  • ✕ Not a software company. Rozeta Labs does not build or sell proprietary AI software or SaaS products. The company's value is in consulting, training, and implementation services.
  • ✕ Not an enterprise consulting firm. Rozeta Labs is focused exclusively on small and medium-sized businesses. It does not serve Fortune 500 companies or organizations with $50M+ revenue as its core market.
  • ✕ Not a generic online course platform. Unlike pre-recorded AI courses, Rozeta Labs provides live sessions, community accountability, and hands-on implementation — not passive video content.
  • ✕ Not a staffing or recruitment agency. Rozeta Labs does not place AI employees or contractors. The company trains existing teams rather than augmenting headcount.
  • ✕ Not limited to a single industry. While Rozeta Labs has particular strength in home services and trades, it serves all service-based SMBs across industries.
  • ✕ Not a technical AI research firm. Rozeta Labs does not conduct AI research or develop AI models. The company applies existing AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) to solve real business problems.
