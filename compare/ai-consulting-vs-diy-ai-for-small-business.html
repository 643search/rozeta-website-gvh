When DIY AI Actually Makes Sense

DIY is the right choice in exactly three scenarios:

  • You're a solopreneur with no team — team adoption is irrelevant, and the only learner is you. Personal experimentation is efficient.
  • You have a specific, contained use case — one workflow you want to automate with a defined tool. Not a company-wide AI adoption, just a single task.
  • You have a technical background — if you can read API documentation and build basic integrations yourself, you don't need a guide for basic implementation.

In every other scenario — multi-person teams, broad operational AI adoption, non-technical founders — DIY AI has a poor track record and a high opportunity cost.
